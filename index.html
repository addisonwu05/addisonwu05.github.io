Hey!

I'm a junior (third-year) at Princeton University studying computer science and math, grateful to be advised by Tom Griffiths, Zhuang Liu, and Peter Henderson. I was born in the Bay Area 🌉 but have lived in Kingston, Ontario, Canada 🇨🇦 for my entire life.

This summer, I will be joining the Center for AI Safety as a Research Intern.

Broadly speaking, I study how foundation models behave under informationally imperfect (unreliable, incomplete, deceptive, conflicting, etc.) yet realistic conditions from train-time to deployment, and the real-world implications of these limitations.

Outside of research, I like to play Wordle, Globle, Travle, GeoGuessr/Geotastic, and the NYT Mini. My life goal is to defeat the Ender Dragon in Minecraft Survival Mode.
